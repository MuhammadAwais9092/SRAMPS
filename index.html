About SRAMPS

Excellence in education since our establishment

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce hard working, responsible and respectful citizens and individuals who strive to excel in all aspects of life with integrity. We accomplish this by creating a conducive and positive environment where all students feel comfortable, respected, and are encouraged for their achievements and successes.

Our Vision

Vision of H.H. Shaikh Rashid Al Maktoum Pakistani School (SRAMPS) is to raise diligent, lifelong learners who are both responsible citizens and respectful individuals.
