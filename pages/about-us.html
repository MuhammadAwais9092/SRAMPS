About SRAMPS

Excellence in education, nurturing future leaders with integrity and values

Welcome to SRAMPS

H.H. Sheikh Rashid Al Maktoum Pakistani School (SRAMPS) stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Dubai, committed to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders.

Established with the vision of providing quality education that combines academic rigor with moral values, SRAMPS has been serving the Pakistani community and international families in the UAE for years. Our school is named in honor of H.H. Sheikh Rashid Al Maktoum, reflecting our deep respect for the UAE's leadership and our commitment to contributing positively to this great nation.

At SRAMPS, we believe that education is not just about academic achievement but about developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of tomorrow with confidence, integrity, and compassion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce hard working, responsible and respectful citizens and individuals who strive to excel in all aspects of life with integrity. We accomplish this by creating a conducive and positive environment where all students feel comfortable, respected, and are encouraged for their achievements and successes.

Our Vision

Vision of H.H. Shaikh Rashid Al Maktoum Pakistani School (SRAMPS) is to raise diligent, lifelong learners who are both responsible citizens and respectful individuals.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Respect

We foster an environment of mutual respect where every individual is valued and their dignity is upheld.

Integrity

We believe in honesty, transparency, and doing the right thing even when no one is watching.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, encouraging continuous improvement and growth.

Responsibility

We encourage students to take ownership of their actions and contribute positively to their community.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas and technologies to enhance learning and prepare students for the future.

Global Citizenship

We prepare students to be responsible global citizens who understand and appreciate diversity.

Our Educational Philosophy

At SRAMPS, we believe that every child is unique and has the potential to excel. Our educational philosophy is built on the foundation of holistic development, ensuring that students grow academically, socially, emotionally, and morally.

Student-Centered Learning: We place students at the center of the learning process, adapting our teaching methods to meet individual needs and learning styles.
Character Development: We emphasize the importance of character building alongside academic achievement, nurturing values that will serve students throughout their lives.
Critical Thinking: We encourage students to think critically, ask questions, and develop problem-solving skills that will prepare them for future challenges.
Cultural Appreciation: We celebrate diversity and help students develop an appreciation for different cultures while maintaining their own cultural identity.
