Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by grade level. Please visit our admissions page or contact our admissions office for detailed information about required documents and the application process.

Do you offer school transportation?

Yes, we provide school bus services to various areas in Dubai and Sharjah. Transportation fees vary by location. Please contact us for specific route information and pricing.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow the Cambridge curriculum for early years and primary education, and the Pakistani Federal Board curriculum for secondary and higher secondary levels.

Can I schedule a school tour?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective families to visit our campus. Please contact our admissions office to schedule a tour at your convenience.
